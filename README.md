# Sanskar's Whiteboard

**Notes on systems, artificial intelligence, interpretability, applied mathematics, and ideas worth exploring.**

Welcome to *Sanskar's Whiteboard*, a technical blog where I explore research ideas and system designs at the intersection of:

- Machine learning and AI
- Systems architecture
- Behavioral analysis of software
- Applied mathematics in computing
- AI-driven cybersecurity
- Interpretability and model understanding

The goal of this blog is to document technical thoughts, experimental ideas, research notes, and conceptual architectures, some of which may later develop into formal research papers.

## Current Series

### Topology-Based Behavioral Malware Detection

An exploration of a conceptual architecture for detecting malware using:

- Behavioral trajectories
- Manifold embeddings
- Persistent homology
- Predictive trajectory modeling
- Reinforcement learning with human feedback

The central idea:

> Malware can mutate its code, but it is much harder to mutate the geometry of its behavior.
